Our services

What We Do

Pest Control Services

  • Regular inspections and treatments to prevent and control pest infestations.
  • Use of non-toxic and eco-friendly pest control methods to ensure patient safety.

Waste Management Services

  • Efficient and safe disposal of general, medical, and hazardous waste.
  • Compliance with environmental and health regulations for waste management.

Security Services

  • Providing trained security personnel to ensure the safety of patients, staff, and visitors.
  • Implementing access control systems and surveillance to monitor sensitive areas.

Laundry and Linen Services

  • Proper handling and laundering of hospital linens and uniforms.
  • Ensuring strict adherence to hygiene standards to prevent contamination.

Cleaning and Housekeeping Services

  • Routine and deep cleaning of patient rooms, operating theaters, and common areas.
  • Infection control cleaning to prevent the spread of healthcare-associated infections (HAIs).
  • Waste management, including hazardous and medical waste disposal.

Environmental Services

  • Monitoring and managing air and water quality within the facility.
  • Implementing energy-efficient practices and sustainability initiatives.
