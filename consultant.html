Dr. Tosin Akeredolu

Executive Chairman of NITORTS Group Ltd.

Dr. Tosin Akeredolu is a distinguished professional in Preventive Veterinary Medicine with a strong academic background and extensive experience in the field. He has made significant contributions to Nigeria's agricultural sector and veterinary services.

Education and Academic Background

Dr. Akeredolu pursued his higher education at the University of California, Davis, specializing in Preventive Veterinary Medicine. His academic journey equipped him with extensive knowledge in veterinary public health, disease prevention, and control strategies.

Professional Experience
  • Director at the Federal Ministry of Agriculture: Dr. Akeredolu shaped policies and implemented programs to improve agricultural practices and ensure food security in Nigeria.
  • Quarantine Services in Nigeria: He played a pivotal role in setting up quarantine services to prevent the introduction and spread of animal diseases, safeguarding both livestock and public health.
Current Role

Currently, Dr. Akeredolu serves as the Executive Chairman of NITORTS Group Ltd., guiding the organization towards substantial growth and innovation.

Personal Attributes

Known for his visionary leadership, strategic thinking, and commitment to excellence, Dr. Akeredolu is a respected figure in veterinary medicine and agriculture, both locally and internationally.
